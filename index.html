Intro

Welcome to my portfolio website! I'm excited to have the opportunity to showcase my skills and projects to you. My name is Martin Ndungu, and I am a software engineering student, specializing in Machine Learning. I have had the pleasure of working on a variety of projects, ranging from full-stack web development to data modelling and visualization. Each project has provided me with the opportunity to hone my skills and expand my knowledge, and I am eager to share my experiences with you. On this website, you will find a collection of my best work, including Flight data analysis, Full-stack web development and more. Each project is accompanied by a brief description, outlining the objectives, challenges, and outcomes. Additionally, you will find information about my professional background, education, and certifications. Thank you for taking the time to visit my portfolio website. I hope you enjoy browsing my work and learning more about me. If you have any questions or would like to discuss potential collaboration opportunities, please don't hesitate to reach out.

About

Martin Ndungu is a Mastercard Foundation scholar at the African Leadership University, pursuing studies in software engineering and entrepreneurship. He is fueled by his passion for social innovation, especially through technology. Martin aims to build on his academic foundations in software engineering and entrepreneurship through continued school work and part-time professional development. His quest for knowledge and value for a positive work ethic has contributed immensely to his recent success at Lyceum Education Consultants (CA) as a research assistant. Here, he has helped conduct and manage research projects within Kenya. Meanwhile, he is aiming to understand the practical nuances of artificial intelligence for social development through a part-time internship in artificial intelligence or small-scale agribusiness scaling
